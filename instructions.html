How to Play

The Kingdom of Thronmore is under siege! You must protect the castle and survive the goblin assault until the time expires. If you do so, you will advance to the next level. Each round lasts ten minutes.

You control both military units and villagers. Villagers can harvest food, wood, and stone. These are things you need to defend your castle. But, be careful, if left undefended, your villagers can easily be killed.

You can create military units out of available resources by pressing Recruit. You move units and villagers by clicking on them and designating where you want them to go. You can create defensive works by pressing Build. But be careful. You only have so many resources. Good luck!
